About Me

I am a CS graduate student at UIUC. I am fortunate to work with Prof. Tong Zhang and Prof. Minjia Zhang at UIUC. My research interests lie in Machine Learning Systems and Deep Generative Models. Specifically, I am excited about using system-algorithm co-design to improve the reasoning abilities of LLMs and LLM agents efficiently.

I love building open-source software, and I am always open to research collaboration. Feel free to contact me via email.
